About Us

Aarhus Ultimate Frisbee Club was founded in 1992. Since the club has changed name numerous times and is now under the name Aarhus Ultimate and is the only Ultimate club in Aarhus. Aarhus Ultimate has the purpose of enhancing social companionship with focus on ultimate in Aarhus. Thereto the klub will raise the sports level to benefit the elite of danish ultimate.
Our womens team are ruling champions of Denmark in both ourdoor and indoor, and has been consistent in the top 2 through many years. While the mens team have been sticking to the top 3 in both outdoor and indoor through the years. The club has the objective to reach top 3 in all division: women, open and mix in both outdoor and indoor.
Apart from the danish championship the club participates in tournaments every year in both Denmark and internationally.

    About Ultimate

    Ultimate is a fairplaysport, which means that there is no referee, but that the players have the sole responsibilty of calling fouls and knowing the rules of the game. Ultimate is a contact-free sport, which is played with a disc.
    A team scores a point by catching the disc in the opposite teams endzone. The players are not allowed to run with the disc, so you rely on being able to transport the disc by throwing it to your teammates. When you are holding the disc, the opposite player covering you will count the "stallcount" in seconds. In outdoor the stallcount is 10 seconds and indoors the stallcount is 8 seconds. In the case that your defender counts to 10 or 8, the disc touches the ground, is caught by and opposite player or goes outside the field, then the oppisite team will gain the disc.


    The Field

    Ourdoors: The field is 100 meters long and 37 meters wide. The endzone is 18 meters long, and the game is played 7 vs 7
    Indoor: The field is at the size of a regular handball field (40m x 20m). The endzone is 6 meters deep, and the game is played 5v5.
