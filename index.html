Sign Up

To become a member of Aarhus Ultimate you first need create an account at Klub Modul and pay the following subscribtions:

  • 300 kr subscription each year.
  • 400 kr activity subscription* each season.
    • After paying the subscribtions send an email to kasserer@aarhusultimate.dk including your name and the day you payed the subscribtions.

    Once the above standing is done. You are now a member of Aarhus Ultimate and you will recieve a text every time the subscribtion will be drawn.

    * This subscription covers transport and players fee to all national and some international tournaments, among other things.
